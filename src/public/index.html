Facilitated Mode

✦

amplify feature

The channel creator can appoint 1~3 facilitator(s) in this mode. The facilitators may use the amplify feature to outline agendas, ask questions and guide conversations.

✦

Amplify Module

Enables facilitator(s) to amplify messages. This feature may be useful for creating agendas and leading questions to guide conversations in the space.

Voting Module

Enables participants to turn any message into a voting poll.

Consentful Gesture Module

Enables participants to provide secondary feedback, such as “agree”, “hesitant”, “disagree”, or “block” on a message.
